ABOUT US



OVERVIEW


Milan is founded in 2017 by Hardik Jain and his friends with a strong passion to work in the field of education.

The organization provides families a platform to contribute to society and bridges the gap between upper middle class and rich familes and the underprivileged children through education.



VISION








Transform society to improve lives nationally through Education



MISSION



To be free in giving

To work holistically for communities less privileged.

To bridge the wealthy families and underprivileged children.

To create partnerships and networks to leverage maximum impact for the beneficiaries.




WHAT WE DO




MILAN is the only non profit organization in India that doesn’t take donations.

We seek to strive for better society by raising the bar of education in India.
MILAN works for education of underprivileged children who can't afford to go school.
We help them by linking upper middle class family with those underprivileged children.

Each Family sponsor one child and pays for their education till their high school/graduation.
Family is monitored every 2-3 month by our team. Education promotes equality and lifts people out of poverty.
It teaches children how to become good citizens. Education is not just for a privileged few, it is for everyone.

It is a fundamental human right
